ABOUT JEEWA AUSTRALIAN EDUCATION CENTRE (JAEC)

Leading Australian Education Consultant in Sri Lanka with more than 15 years of experience.

Our genesis heads back to 17 years ago, when we pioneered the idea of bridging the gaps between the universities of repute in Australia and the Sri Lankan students who aspire to pursue the esteemed levels of higher education in the greener pastures of Australia.

At Jeewa Education, we define our offering on the premises of a personalized service that matches your individual needs, unequivocal and friendly guidance from start to end, and the lucid administering of extensive information, which altogether will help defy all obstacles and myths that stall your academic odyssey to Australia.

Our experts will proffer you advice on the plethora of Australian universities, the programs offered and the fundamentals of the admission processes, which will assuredly guide you to making an informed decision. Our eccentricity was pronounced, when we spearheaded a makeover in the Australian Education Consultancy Services in Sri Lanka by initiating a manifold of value added onshore services including securing discounted air ticket prices for students, arranging airport pickups, facilitating accommodation placements and organizing onshore student networking events. The roots of Jeewa Group have also been extended to Australia with the opening of the two onshore offices in Melbourne and Brisbane.

In synopsis, the well-entrenched DNA of Jeewa Education Group lies in providing an all-inclusive service that helps students actualize their aspirations of studying in Australia.
